About Me

I am a rising junior at New York University's College of Arts & Science, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Business Studies. Growing up on Long Island, NY, with immigrant parents of Chinese descent, my journey has been shaped by their influence and my drive for success.

My aspiration is to become a software engineer, and I have been working diligently to acquire the necessary skills to excel in this field. Technology has always captivated my interest, and from a young age, I found joy in providing IT support to family and friends. My fascination with computer science began at the age of 10 when I discovered a game called "MapleStory." This experience ignited my curiosity and led me to create my private server and website using online guides and C++ templates. Ever since then, computer science has become a gateway to explore my two passions: problem-solving and crafting innovative experiences.

The process of envisioning an experience, designing potential solutions, and bringing them to life through coding brings me immense satisfaction. I find joy in transforming ideas into tangible solutions that address specific user challenges. It is this end-to-end journey that fuels my passion for computer science and motivates me to continually push the boundaries of what is possible. I am excited about the future and the opportunities that lie ahead, and I cannot wait to make a meaningful impact in the world through technology. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website!
